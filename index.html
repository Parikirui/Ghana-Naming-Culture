About Us

This is a computing system that is used in Ghana to give names to children based on the days that they were born. There exist the various names that are associated with the different days of the week in Ghana. This system necessitate the user to enter his date of birth as well as the gender. After that, he or she will be required to click the submit button. It is then that the system will bring the cultural names that are associated with the users.
